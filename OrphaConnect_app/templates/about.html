About Us

OrphaConnect is a pioneering web-based platform dedicated to addressing the dual challenges of food waste and supporting orphanages. Our platform connects donors with orphanages in need, facilitating the donation process and fostering community engagement.

How It Works?

OrphaConnect streamlines the donation process by allowing donors to post surplus food availability details, triggering real-time notifications to nearby orphanages. The platform guides users through the donation process, ensuring transparency and authenticity.

Key Features

Real-time notifications for surplus food availability. Secure authentication system to verify donor identity. Reward-based incentive system to encourage sustained contributions. Robust security measures to prevent fraudulent activities. User-friendly interface for seamless navigation and engagement.
